一、作品特点及用途
Work features and USES

####（一）特点：

####Features:

  • （1）使用可更换光显和磁显两种显示装置，跨学科远距离显示；

  • The two kinds of display devices can be replaced with both optical and magnetic display.

  • （2）精致的电解瓶，将各种典型的电解液按合适浓度提前配制密封，一插即用；

  • The delicate electrolytic bottle, which will be sealed in advance by the appropriate concentration of all the typical electrolyte, in a plug and play;

  • （3）独立开发的固体电解管，实现了课堂演示低温固体融化后测试导电能力的目标。

  • The independent development of solid electrolytic tube has achieved the objective of testing the conductive capability after the classroom demonstration of low temperature solid melting.

  • （4）整套装置体积小巧，真正实现实验微型化，为教师赢得更多时间和空间。

  • The whole apparatus is small, and the experiment is miniaturized to win more time and space for teachers.

####（二）用途：本作品可完成以下化学实验

####Use: this work can complete the following chemistry experiments

  • （1）测定电解质和非电解质

  • Determination of electrolyte and non-electrolyte

  • （2）测定强弱电解质

  • Determination of strong and weak electrolytes

  • （3）测定熔融状态固体电解质导电性

  • Determine the conductivity of molten solid electrolyte

二、制作材料

####Make materials

3D打印树脂、LED、电位器一个、PCB电路板、LM3914 点/条显示驱动集成电路、漆包线、、单刀三掷开关、铜棒、铜线、电烙铁、锡条、USB接头/口、DC接头/口等

3D printing resin, leds, potentiometer, an article, PCB circuit boards, LM3914 point/display driver IC, enameled wire, and single pole, three throw switch, copper rod, copper wire, soldering iron, tin, USB/mouth, DC connector/mouth

三、制作方法

####Making methods

笔身

####Pen body

（1）设计电路；导线连接电池盒正负极、DC接口/头、USB接口/头、开关。
Design circuit; The wire connects the battery box to the negative pole, DC interface/head, USB interface/head, switch.

（2）开关分配导线；将分配完成的导线连接USB接口。
Switch distribution conductor; Connect the completed wire to the USB port.

（3）在计算机上设计外壳，通过3D打印制作，并手工组装。
Design the shell on the computer, make it by 3D printing, and assemble it by hand.

（4）将3D打印的外壳与内部电路组装，用胶水粘贴牢固。
Assemble the 3D printed shell with the internal circuit, and stick it firmly with glue.
