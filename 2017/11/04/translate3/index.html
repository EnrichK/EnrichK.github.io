Work is Introduced

发表于 2017-11-04

一、Work features and USES

Features:

  • （1） The two kinds of display devices can be replaced with both optical and magnetic display.

  • （2）The delicate electrolytic bottle, which will be sealed in advance by the appropriate concentration of all the typical electrolyte, in a plug and play;

  • （3）The independent development of solid electrolytic tube has achieved the objective of testing the conductive capability after the classroom demonstration of low temperature solid melting.

  • （4）The whole apparatus is small, and the experiment is miniaturized to win more time and space for teachers.

Use: This work can complete the following chemistry experiments

  • （1）Determination of electrolyte and non-electrolyte

  • （2）Determination of strong and weak electrolytes

  • （3） Determine the conductivity of molten solid electrolyte
    Make materials

Make materials

3D printing resin, leds, potentiometer, an article, PCB circuit boards, LM3914 point/display driver IC, enameled wire, and single pole, three throw switch, copper rod, copper wire, soldering iron, tin, USB/mouth, DC connector/mouth

Making methods

Pen body

（1）Design circuit; The wire connects the battery box to the negative pole, DC interface/head, USB interface/head, switch.

（2）Switch distribution conductor; Connect the completed wire to the USB port.

（3）Design the shell on the computer, make it by 3D printing, and assemble it by hand.

（4）Assemble the 3D printed shell with the internal circuit, and stick it firmly with glue.
