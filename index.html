发表于 2017-11-04

新型化学电笔微盒作为教学仪器，其目的是帮助学生正确理解电解质、非电解质、强电解质、弱电解质的概念。

Use the Instruction Manual

发表于 2017-11-04

The new chemical pen microbox as a teaching instrument is designed to help students understand the concept of electrolyte, non-electrolyte, strong electrolyte and weak electrolyte.

The product can be replaced by two kinds of display devices, such as optical display and magnetic display. The delicate electrolytic bottle is prepared by the proper concentration of various typical electrolytes. The independent development of solid electrolytic tube has achieved the objective of testing the conductive capability after the class demonstration of low temperature solid melting.
